SteamBird is the replacement for the ABC (Arjan's Book Companion) system of IAPC

For years the book committee of IAPC together with the study associations Inter-Actief and Proto made sure all book and course information is up to date in order for students to be able to buy the right books for their courses. Over the years, this has led to the development of the ABC system that aids the book committee in doing its task. Steambird is supposed to be a more mature and more modern service to replace the dated ABC system.
